WB Police Constable Recruitment 2024

The WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination is conducted by WBPRB. The recruitment process for WB Police Constable encompasses a written examination, a physical test, and an interview. Successful completion of these stages ensures consideration for the coveted position of WB Police Constable. This page contains all essential details about the forthcoming WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination 2024.

WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination 2024: Application Process

 Candidates need to visit the official website of the West Bengal Police Recruitment Board (WBPRB) to apply for the WB Police Constable post

To proceed with the application process for WB Police Constable Recruitment 2024, kindly follow the steps provided below:

  1. Visit the Official website of WBPRB 
  2. Locate and click on the “Apply Online” button situated on the WBPRB Homepage.
  3. In case you haven’t registered already, complete the registration process.
  4. Thoroughly read all the instructions before proceeding with the application.
  5. Give all the required details meticulously, and also upload your photograph, signature, and other necessary documents.
  6. Make the payment for the application fee.
  7. After ensuring all details are correct, submit the application form, and remember to keep a printout of the application form and fee receipt for future reference. 

WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination 2024: Application Fee

As part of the online application process, candidates are required to pay the Application Fee. The following table outlines the application fee for various categories:

WB Police Constable Application Fee:

  • UR Category: Application Fee – 150 INR, Processing Fee – 20 INR, Total – 170 INR
  • SC/ST (of WB only): Application Fee – Not Applicable, Processing Fee – 20 INR, Total – 20 INR (Note: There is no exemption of fee for SC/ST candidates from other states.)

It’s important to note that the application fee is non-refundable.  Any application submitted without the appropriate fee payment will be rejected. The application fee can be paid through UPI-enabled payment gateways, Internet banking, and debit/credit cards as per the convenience of the applicants.

WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination 2024: Eligibility Criteria

Candidates need to meet the required Eligibility Criteria as outlined by WBPRB in order to sit for the WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination.  Firstly they  must be Indian citizens, and secondly both their physical and mental fitness must be sufficient to fulfill their duties effectively as a Police Constable. 

Additionally, the age of candidates should fall within the range of 18 to 27 years, and they must have successfully qualified the Madhyamik Examination from a recognized educational board. Furthermore, proficiency in the Bengali language is required to get selected for the job.

WB Police Constable Recruitment 2024: Exam Pattern

The WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination follows a specific pattern. The exam is divided into three sections, namely:

  • General Studies, consisting of 50 questions carrying 50 marks, with a total time of 60 minutes.
  • Numerical & Mental Ability Test, comprising of  30 questions carrying 30 marks.
  • Logical & Analytical Reasoning, with 20 questions carrying 20 marks.

In total, there are 100 questions for 100 marks in the WB Police Constable Preliminary Exam.

On the other hand, the WB Police Constable Mains Exam consists of four sections:

  • English, with 25 questions carrying 25 marks and a duration of 1 hour.
  • General Awareness & GK, including 25 questions for 25 marks.
  • Mathematics, comprising 20 questions carrying 20 marks.
  • Reasoning, with 15 questions for 15 marks.

In summary, the WB Police Constable Mains Exam comprises a total of 85 questions carrying 85 marks.

WB Police Constable Recruitment Examination 2024: Salary Details

The in hand salary of a WB Police Constable is in accordance with Government regulations. The pay scale for WB Police Constable ranges from Rs. 22,500 to Rs. 58,500 per month, and it includes a grade pay of Rs. 2,600. Apart from the salary, selected candidates are entitled to various allowances such as House Rent Allowance (HRA), Dearness Allowance (DA), etc. This presents an excellent opportunity for aspiring candidates.
